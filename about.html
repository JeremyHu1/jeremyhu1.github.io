About Us
SuperMemo is a learning method and software developed by SuperMemo World with leadership from Piotr Wozniak from Poland. Piotr Wozniak is best known for his research in creating and propagating the concept of spaced repetition. In addition to his theory of spaced repetition, Piotr also is interested in incremental reading and free schooling.
The forgetting curve illustrates how memory retention declines over time without reinforcement. SuperMemo is a software based on this concept, using spaced repetition to optimize learning. It schedules review sessions based on the forgetting curve to maximize long-term memory retention. Users input information, and SuperMemo determines the optimal timing for reviewing it, helping learners remember more efficiently.
